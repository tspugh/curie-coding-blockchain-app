Adalimumab — Specialty (Tier 5)

Status: Covered with restrictions — prior authorization, step therapy, quantity limit.

Prior authorization criteria

  • Indication: moderate-to-severe chronic plaque psoriasis.
  • Disease severity: BSA ≥ 10%, or PASI ≥ 12, or sensitive-area involvement.
  • Trial-and-failure or intolerance of at least one preferred conventional systemic therapy (e.g. methotrexate).
  • No active serious infection; latent TB screening performed.

Step-therapy exception

An exception applies when a preferred conventional systemic agent has been tried and failed, or is contraindicated/not tolerated, with documentation.

Benchmark price band

Floor 2800 — Ceiling 5200 (unitless marker).

Ruling guidance

Return approve when the prior-auth criteria and a valid step-therapy exception are documented; need_more_evidence when documentation is incomplete; deny when a preferred agent has not been tried and no exception applies.
