# USDC on Somnia: Native CCTP vs. Bridged USDC.e — Settlement Token Trust Model

## 2026-05-15 — Has Circle applied to add Somnia to CCTP V2, and what is the trust model difference between native and bridged USDC for hospital-payer settlement?

- **No Circle-Somnia CCTP integration exists or has been announced as of May 2026.** CCTP V2 supports 25 mainnet/testnet chains (Ethereum, Arbitrum, Base, Optimism, Polygon PoS, Avalanche, Solana, Linea, Unichain, Sonic, World Chain, Monad, Sei, Starknet, XDC, HyperEVM, Injective, Morph, Pharos, EDGE, Ink, Plume, Codex, Arc, Stellar testnet). Somnia does not appear on this list, and no public announcement of a CCTP V2 pipeline application for Somnia exists. Sources: [Circle CCTP supported chains](https://developers.circle.com/cctp/cctp-supported-blockchains), [CCTP V2 canonical announcement](https://www.circle.com/blog/cctp-version-updates).

- **The USDC on Somnia mainnet (contract `0x28BEc7E30E6faee657a03e19Bf1128AaD7632A00`) is Stargate-bridged USDC.e, not Circle-native USDC.** Explorer title confirms: "Somnia Mainnet USDC.e token details." Market cap ~$1.58M, 24-hour volume ~$1.15M (as of May 2026) — thin institutional liquidity. Sources: [Somnia explorer token](https://explorer.somnia.network/token/0x28BEc7E30E6faee657a03e19Bf1128AaD7632A00), [DropsTab](https://dropstab.com/coins/stargate-bridged-usdc-somnia).

- **USDC.e (Stargate-bridged) carries material counterparty and smart contract risk that native USDC does not.** Bridges accounted for the majority of crypto exploits in 2022 (Wormhole $320M Feb 2022, Nomad $190M Aug 2022 were both lock-and-mint bridge exploits). USDC.e requires trusting Stargate/LayerZero operators; native USDC requires only trusting Circle. Source: [usdc.com USDC vs USDC.e](https://www.usdc.com/learn/whats-the-difference-between-usdc-and-usdce).

- **Circle's Bridged USDC Standard offers a structured upgrade path from bridged to native issuance.** Under this standard, a chain team deploys a Circle-audited bridged token contract, bootstraps liquidity, achieves high adoption, and then Circle may exercise the option to take ownership and upgrade to native USDC in-place (no developer code changes required). This is distinct from Stargate-bridged USDC.e, which does not use the Bridged USDC Standard and therefore has no formal upgrade pathway to native USDC. Sources: [Circle Bridged USDC Standard](https://www.circle.com/bridged-usdc), [Bridged USDC Standard blog](https://www.circle.com/blog/bridged-usdc-standard).

- **The GENIUS Act (signed July 18, 2025) creates a "permitted payment stablecoin issuer" (PPSI) framework, but does not explicitly regulate bridged tokens.** GENIUS Act compliance is attributed to Circle as the PPSI for native USDC. Bridged USDC.e is issued by Stargate/LayerZero (not a PPSI), which means it does not carry Circle's regulatory compliance status. Hospital and payer legal counsel reviewing cliqueue's Settlement Token Policy will almost certainly require a PPSI-compliant token. Effective enforcement date: January 18, 2027 (18 months from enactment) or 120 days after final implementing rules. Sources: [Circle GENIUS Act page](https://www.circle.com/genius-act), [Federal Register GENIUS Act FDIC rules](https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2026/04/10/2026-06974/genius-act-requirements-and-standards-for-fdic-supervised-permitted-payment-stablecoin-issuers-and).

- **Polymarket's February 2026 migration from USDC.e (Polygon) to native USDC is a direct precedent.** Polymarket transitioned away from bridged USDC explicitly citing "capital efficiency, scalability, and institutional alignment." This validates the native-vs-bridged distinction as a live enterprise procurement concern, not just a technical nuance. Source: [The Block — Polymarket native USDC](https://www.theblock.co/post/388670/polymarket-to-swap-bridged-usdc-e-on-polygon-for-native-usdc-via-circle-partnership).

- **CCTP V2 roadmap for 2026 shows steady chain expansion (Stellar, Hedera, Aptos, Sui in pipeline) but no EVM L1 gaming/AI chains comparable to Somnia.** Somnia would need to either (a) apply to Circle directly for CCTP V2 integration, (b) adopt the Bridged USDC Standard with a Somnia-native bridge contract, or (c) continue with Stargate USDC.e. The CCTP V2 pipeline expansion targets Stellar and Hedera as of the January 2026 Circle roadmap. Source: [eco.com CCTP V2 guide](https://eco.com/support/en/articles/11813797-circle-cctp-v2-native-usdc-across-13-chains).
